# subconscious

**Executive intelligence layer for Sanders Software Solutions** — adversarial thinking, structured research, and external-facing document production for a sole founder operating at portfolio scale.

## Skills

### red-team
Adversarial review skill that stress-tests any plan, decision, architecture, or strategy from the specific context of the SSS subsidiary being evaluated. Triggers on: "challenge this", "what am I missing", "poke holes", "red team this", "stress test", "devil's advocate", "what could go wrong".

### due-diligence
Structured research analyst that produces competitive landscape, market size, and opportunity assessments. Use when evaluating a new market, competitor, partnership, or investment thesis.

### external-deliverables
Produces documents that leave Sanders Software Solutions and land in front of clients, partners, investors, or the public. Governs tone, structure, and quality standards for all external-facing output.
